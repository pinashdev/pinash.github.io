About us

Incense has been holding a prominent position on religious occasions and other sacred events for thousands of years. The fragrance of incense enveloping a temple, a home or any place generate a divine ambience signifying peace, health, prosperity and progeny. Sun Agarbatti & Co. is a pioneer and accomplished manufacturer and exporter of incense, incense sticks, dhoop sticks, cones, agarbatti and other related items since the past several decades. The entity was incorporated in the year 1955 and is a successor to Shah Agarbatti & Co. The existence of the company as an undisputed conqueror for more than five decades itself explains the quality worth of its products. The company is one of the major feeder of the global incense demand.

The company is keenly administered by sound management principles to deliver at par with the best fragrance solutions. The entity is known for its reliability, quality and timely delivery of the products. Facility of factory stuffing of containers is used to prevent the products from damage and pilferage. World class packing retains the fragrance for a longer period of time. Every product passes through a series of severe inspection to ensure flawless delivery of products. Finest quality of raw materials such as spent wood dust, sandal powder, etc. are used to manufacture superlative products.
